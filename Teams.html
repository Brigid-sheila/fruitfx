Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team at FruitFix Farm works tirelessly to provide the best quality, fresh, and organic fruits. Each team member brings a unique set of skills and expertise to ensure that we are not only meeting but exceeding customer expectations.

James Green

Founder & CEO

James is the visionary behind FruitFix Farm. With over 20 years of experience in sustainable farming and organic produce, he founded FruitFix Farm to provide fresh, locally grown fruits to the community. His passion for agriculture and commitment to eco-friendly farming practices have earned him recognition in the sustainable farming industry. James holds a degree in Agricultural Science from the University of California, Davis, and is a member of the Organic Farming Association.

Linda Martinez

Chief Operations Officer

Linda oversees the day-to-day operations at FruitFix Farm, ensuring that everything runs smoothly. With over 15 years of experience in agricultural logistics and supply chain management, Linda is responsible for streamlining processes, managing inventory, and coordinating with distributors. Her expertise in farm-to-table logistics ensures that our customers receive fresh produce every time. Linda holds an MBA in Supply Chain Management from Stanford University.

Michael Brown

Farm Manager

Michael is in charge of managing the farming operations at FruitFix. He works closely with the farming team to ensure that crops are grown according to the highest standards of quality and sustainability. With a background in horticulture and organic farming techniques, Michael is passionate about developing innovative approaches to farming. He has been with the farm for 10 years and is known for his expertise in soil health and crop rotation.

Rachel Green

Marketing Director

Rachel is responsible for shaping and executing the marketing strategy at FruitFix Farm. She leverages her expertise in digital marketing and social media to build awareness about the farm's commitment to providing fresh, organic fruits. Rachel's creative campaigns have helped FruitFix establish a loyal customer base and attract new business. She holds a degree in Marketing from the University of Florida and has over 10 years of experience in the industry.

David Lee

Head of Sales

David leads the sales team at FruitFix Farm, building strong relationships with retailers, distributors, and local markets. His ability to understand customer needs and tailor solutions has been key to expanding the farm’s reach across regions. With a background in business development and sales strategy, David has played a pivotal role in growing the company’s market presence. He holds a degree in Business Administration from Harvard University and is passionate about sustainability in the food industry.
